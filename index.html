How It Works

The Scholarship Lifecycle, Fully Automated

01

Student Registers

Student submits their name and ID. Their MetaMask wallet is auto-captured on-chain as the payment address.

→
02

Provider Funds

Provider sends ETH and sets minimum attendance % and average mark. Funds are locked in the Staff escrow contract.

→
03

Staff Submits Results

University staff enters the student's actual attendance and grade. The smart contract evaluates instantly.

→
04

Auto Disbursement

Criteria met → ETH transferred directly to student wallet. Not met → marked failed. Cancelled → provider refunded.
